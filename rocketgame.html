Rocket Game

Rocket Game | Concept | Levels 1-6 

Download Rocket Game on Itch.io!

In Rocket Game, you use a low-poly rocket to traverse through structured levels that will sometimes rely on your environment such as using buttons, avoiding the lasers that shoot at you and solving some sequences and puzzles!

You can also damage/break your rocket if you ram into a wall or floor too hard or fly into random objects at high velocity, that's why you should take it nice, slow and easy when traversing these testing rooms!

Your objective is simple, you fly the rocket and land on your landing point while completing the required tasks. Are you up to the challenge?
